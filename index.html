Dear CEO:

This is the overall combined sales of our food products across all regions (North East, North West, South East, South West, Central). As you can see, unfortunately, our hamburgers' sales had a significant drop in October 2018, along with minor decreases of the chicken and fish fillets, probably because of the introduction of the Impossible Burger by Burger King. Judging from the data, it was a negative impact for our company's business. While the sales of the other products remained quite stable afterwards, our hamburgers had yet not recovered from this impact, and are unlikely to return to its peak at any time soon. But while this was indeed a tragic defeat for our company, this is also a good strategy that we should adopt for a revenge. I hereby propose the corporation to carefully consider designing a new McDonald's product that will strike for a big hit in the market in order to restore our sales.
